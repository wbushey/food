Gluten Free Banana Bread

Gluten Free

Time to Prepare: 65 minutes

Number of Servings: 24

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup rice flour
  • heaping 1/3 cup potato starch
  • heaping 1/4 cup tapioca flour
  • 2/3 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups or 4-5 medium bananas
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup, chopped walnuts
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking soda and salt. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs, mashed bananas, sugar, applesauce, oil and vanilla. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.
  3. Transfer to two 8-in. x 4-in. loaf pans coated with cooking spray. Sprinkle with walnuts. Bake at 350Â° for 45-55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. Yield: 2 loaves (12 slices each).

Notes: Also works as muffins! And you can add chocolate too. I replaced the 2 cups gf flour mix with roughly what I use.
