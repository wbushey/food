Orange Marinaded Pork Chops

Gluten Free

Time to Prepare: 30 minutes

Number of Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • to taste black pepper
  1. Crush garlic. Make orange juice if neccessary.
  2. Combine orange juice, garlic, olive oil, cumin, and black pepper to create marinade.
  3. Marinade pork chops for at least 2 hours.
  4. Cook pork chops.
