Wendy's Beef Chilie

Gluten Free

Time to Prepare: 3.5 hours

Number of Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground beef
  • 29 oz tomato sauce
  • 29 oz kidney beans
  • 29 oz pinto beans
  • 1 cup onion
  • 2 green chili
  • 1 celery
  • 3 tomatoe
  • 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1.5 teaspoons black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups water
  1. Prep the veggies.
  2. Dice the onion, green chilie, and celery.
  3. Chop tomatoes.
  4. Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat; drain off the fat.
  5. Saute the onions.
  6. In a large pot, combine the beef plus all remaining ingredients, and bring to a simmer over low heat. Cook, stirring every 15 minutes, for 2 to 3 hours.

For spicier chilie, add .5 teaspoons more black powder.

For much spicier chilie, add 1 teaspoon black pepper and 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper.

For a real stomach stinger, add 6 sliced jalapeno peppers to the pot.
