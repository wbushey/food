---
title: Sushi - Spicy Tuna Roll
ingredients:
  nori: 1 sheet
  sushi rice: 1/3 cup
  sushi vinegar:
  tuna: 1 can
  mayonnaise: 
gluten-free: true
time: 1 hour
servings: 1
---

1. Cook the rice.
2. Mix the tuna and mayonnaise
3. Slice any veggies 
4. When rice is cooked, add sushi vinegar.
5. Place rice, tuna, and veggies on nori. Wet one end of the nori, then make a roll.
