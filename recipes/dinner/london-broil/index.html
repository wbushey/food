London Broil

Gluten Free

Time to Prepare: 30 minutes

Number of Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 lbs. flank steak
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp - gluten-free soy salt
  1. In a bowl, mix together chopped garlic, salt, soy sauce, ketchup, oil, pepper, and oregano.
  2. Aerate steak.
  3. Place steak and marinade in bag. Let sit for 2 hours to overnight.
  4. Grill steak.
