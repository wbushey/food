Sweet Potato Crust Pizza

Gluten Free

Time to Prepare: 60 minutes

Number of Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 medium to large sweet potato
  • 1.5 cups (approximate) rice flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2-3 eggs
  • dash salt and pepper
  • 1 can pizza sauce
  • as desired pizza toppings
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cook sweet potato until soft- ~10 min on high in microwave, or roast in oven
  3. When cool enough, skin and mash sweet potato
  4. Slowly add rice flour and baking powder until dough consistency
  5. Beat eggs into dough; it will form a kind of thick paste
  6. Use spatula to spread dough-paste evenly on parchment paper lined baking sheet
  7. Spread sauce on top, place toppings, and put in oven for ~20-40 min

Notes: Different types of sweet potatoes spread differently; I've had the best luck with the bright orange ones.
